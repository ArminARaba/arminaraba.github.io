Situation

In the third week, we got the task to make our Future Dream Sketch, to know what direction we would go in the future, but due everything is still far-far away, therefore we had two indicators for this. First, decide what are our long-term goals in our life, and also the short ones.
But at first, I misunderstood the assignment, and I only made a video about my long-term goals. I consulted about it with Martin, my mentor and he told me that I have to also talk about the short-term goals also.
Due to the deadline, I didn't have that much time to re-edit my video, therefore I decided to write a document. 
Therefore, there will be a video and a pdf file as well.

Task

I made a video of my Future Drean Sketch, where I talked about my past just to reflect on that, and tell with it, what future job possibilities I have.
There was also the short-term goals document file, also, where I wrote down what are my current weaknesses and what I would improve on during my studies.

Action

It was hard at first to imagine what I would tell in the sketch, but because I'm really ambitious about my future possibilities, and I'm a hard-working person, I can get through whatever problem I get in life.

Result

The result.. was kinda boring for me, because I made a 6-minute-long video and a pdf file about my life. But it was good, because I love editing, and doing creative stuff, so it was well enough for me to call all these things my Future Dream Sketch.
I sent these files to my Mentor, Martin, to get feedback from him about it but he didn't reply back till the time I write it. Maybe I misunderstood the assignment, I don't know.


Reflection

Next time, maybe I can be a presentation because the video was so long, and the pdf file is kinda short. So, I'm looking forward to improving on these in the future.
