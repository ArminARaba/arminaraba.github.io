Diagnostic Language Test

STARR

Situation

We had to take a mandatory English language test to see what level we were on, If I know right, the only reason we made this, was to know our level, and if we have weaknesses(of course we do, who doesn't), we can apply to Inspirational Sessions. Where we can develop our skills.


Task

I took the test, it was nothing special to write about it more.

Action

It was kinda easy, but at the same time confusing, because some of the questions were in Dutch, so I lost a little time, figuring out the questions.

Result

It was a good result, I mean, I expected better, to be closer to C1, but at least, I'm still fine with B2.
If I could have slept more, and don't have these kinds of question issues, I would have earned a better level, I think.


Reflection

Due to my result and experience, I decided to join a Conversational group, which will start in the 5th week. Because I have really great English in understanding the context, writing, and reading, but my weakest part is speaking, so I would improve on that lot.
