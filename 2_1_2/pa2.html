Title

Sharing of Shields

Content

STARR

Situation

It was the first assignment we got as a class, to make a visual presentation about ourselves, including our family, and relatives who are close to us.
There is also the cultural side which has to be included in our own national stuff, like a flag, food, places we think are valu,able and the value we give to the world as a nation.
The third one was about the influence we got from our culture, family, it was the place where I showed the most passion I have at the current time, because to due my family's thinking, they always allowed me to do whatever I like. So therefore of my wide interest in almost everything, I had so many hobbies. I had the chance to try out so many different field in the world, starting with filmmaking, photography, music producing, journalism, graphics designing, event organizing, and so on.
The fourth section should be filled with our shared values, I mean by meeting different cultures, and making new friendships around the world.

After all these, I had to tell Tessa (My Do) the whole story of my life, the details of my "sharing of shields", to give her an insight into my life, because she had to present it in front of the class. On the other hand, I had to do it as well, learning about her life, to present her life as well to the class.


Task

At first, I had to find a template to fill my stuff in, I wanted to make it as creative as I can, so, therefore, I decided to edit in Photoshop, because I use this software weekly, and I have several years of experience with it, so it was exciting and fun for me to make it.

Action

It was a really fun project, in my opinion, I liked it so much, of course, it was also stressful, because it was my first assignment, and I didn't know what to expect in the following time. But the development went so well.
I also spent so many hours with Tessa to learn about her life, which was so interesting also, because she was from another culture, and kind to talk with.

Result

The presentation was also stressful, it was also interesting to hear about my life from another person, but when I had to present her life, I freeze and I told her life so fast, in a monotonous way. At that time, I didn't really feel that comfortable with the class, I didn't even speak as well as now I do.
After all, my classmates told me to express myself better, and do gestures with my hands to make it more interesting. They didn't really talk about my speech, probably because they didn't want to hurt me, but I knew, that I had to improve. But, I still need to, so that's why I'm here at the university, to learn more about all these things and improve as much, as I can.


Reflection
The whole project was fun, I liked it, it didn't was that hard, and I had the chance to show my creativity to others through the presentations, which was so great. I have also got the chance to try out myself as a presenter, but as you read and know, I still have a lot to improve on.
Furthermore, I met a very kind girl, named Tessa, who I talk with till these days also.
