Demonstrates insight into development of his/her own knowledge, skills, and attitude. 

 

You show that you have insight into the development of your own knowledge, skills and attitude by reflecting on yourself, your studies and your career.

Overall, the first 4 weeks have been very exciting for me personally, because we had to do so many different assignments. It was very colorful. During this time, I learned the importance of management, which includes the tasks I did in my projects. I couldn't do anything if I don't start organizing the things I should do.
I can also mention time management, which is a corner point. I actually really trying to improve on it, because I can't really manage it, still neither in my l life nor at home.
So, it would useful to learn and get better, because it will help me during my studies, to be more relaxed which leads to a better performance in my studying.

You can work together in a team, take on different roles and are aware of the differences within the group.

Management also applies to teamwork as well, but there is also communication. These are the most important parts of managing to make a product within a team. Without that, we couldn't even continue with our jobs and tasks.
I actually suffered a lot with it, cause in the onboarding assignment my teammates were lazy and inactive in replying back on our social channels. So I lost so much time because of these misunderstandings on their side.
But, of course, all of us are here to learn, so I'm looking forward to developing on these sections as well because I would also improve.

Describes points of development based on a process of gaining experience, collecting feedback, and reflection. 

 

You can give and receive feedback.

At first, it was a bit embarassaing to give feedback to the others, because I didn't know them well. So, I didn't want to say bad things to them. But later on, when we learned how is feedback forming the way of thinking about something, can help a lot.

On the other side, I still try to analyze the things I have to, do to give collective, useful feedback. But, I1m still embarrassed speaking loud in class, but when I will reach the level of comfort I need, I'm sure I will be able to help others with my point of view.

You can describe points of development in a structured reflection based on experiences and feedback

Because everyone's way of thinking is different, therefore, we can collect so much different feedbacks, which can apply to so many different places. Like, talking about my Business Model You, I've got so many different compliments or suggestions, which really helped me a lot, to think differently. Or also, there was the onboarding assignment as well. 
So, if I get the feedback, I can evaluate it as well, and try to think of how to use it to improve my product. It is called prototyping, so, until I don't feel it great, it's not done. I mean, I should always communicate with the feedbackers, even if none of my classmates is available, I can even ask my friends, and my family members, to review my work.
