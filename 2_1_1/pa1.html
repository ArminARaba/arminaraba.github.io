Title

Business Model You

Content

STARR

Situation

The Business Model You PoL was announced on the second week of the base camp, where we had to fill out this kind of document, the business model for myself, about my life and jobs, and the passions I have. 


Task

I had to fill out the business model, to see, what I have done, which fields I'm strong in, and what I can improve on,
Also, we had to share it with our classmates, friends, and family members.

Action

It was a little too confusing for me, to figure out what should I do first, because in a planning way, I'm not that visual type of person, so I had no idea, how to implement my ideas and life into the Busines Model You template. Therefore, I wrote a long document, and after all, I have done with this, had the chance to really find the fields and keywords I can put on the template.
So, that's why you can find two different documents in the attachments. The first one is the "The_Personal_Business_Model_Canvas_AAR_V1_R1_Details.pdf ", this includes all the information I wrote, and there is also the "The_Personal_Business_Model_Canvas_AAR_V1_R3.pdf" which has a more visual, understandable view of my person.

Result

I received so much great feedback about the structure of my BMY, but for some people it was confusing.
I've also got feedback on the visuals, like the font size, and the style.
But due I had limitations on the space in my template, I couldn't change the font size of the notes they suggested. So, therefore, I try to remember these things and do much better in my next assignment.

For some people, it felt like more a biography stylish document, than a BMY.
But, due to the last few months, I have been preparing for my studies so hard, therefore I wrote several CVs, so maybe that's why I have this kind of influence on my writing style. I would lose it because sometimes it makes me hard to express myself other than in a formal way.

So, these feedbacks really helped my BMY to think differently about it and improve on it.


Reflection

It was an interesting assignment, at first, I hated to do it, because I had no idea, what I was doing, but later when I got some insights into the further development of the document, I felt more relaxed about it. Also, it was great to learn that it is almost an industry standard document, which is used by many companies, people who want to reflect on their work like to see, what are they doing and what can they improve on.
It was also really valuable for me as well because I have a motorsport news site in Hungary, so the knowledge I got through this assignment, will be useful for my project, and other future stuff I will do both at the university and in my life.
